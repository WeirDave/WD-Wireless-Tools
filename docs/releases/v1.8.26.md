Two visual tweaks: more breathing room under the dashboard + 1 px grey outline on each row card.

Suite `v1.8.25 → v1.8.26`. WD Cloud Manager `v3.35 → v3.36`.

## 1. More space between dashboard and toolbar row

I misread the earlier feedback in v1.8.25 and made the space too tight. Dave drew on a screenshot to show what he actually wanted — a clear white gap between the dashboard count-cards and the search row.

Fix:
- `.dashboard` padding: `6px 12px 4px → 8px 12px 14px`
- `body.tool-cloud .toolbar` padding: `3px 12px → 8px 12px`

Result:
- **Space between dashboard bottom and search bar top: 10 px** (was 5 px in v1.8.25, 12 px in v1.8.24).
- **Toolbar height: 45 px** (was 35 px), giving the search / delete-all / Live / gear buttons a proper strip instead of feeling cramped.
- Symmetric spacing inside the toolbar: search bar 10 px above / 11 px below, delete-all button 9.2 px above / 10.2 px below.

## 2. 1 px grey border on top and bottom of each row card

Row cards now have `border-top: 1px solid var(--border)` and `border-bottom: 1px solid var(--border)`. Left and right sides are already the 8 px colored bookends (`--blue` for cloud, `--green` for local), so the row now reads as a fully-framed card:

- Top and bottom: 1 px `--border` (subtle grey — `#273240` dark, `#dfe2e6` light).
- Left and right: 8 px blue or green bookends.
- Amber inset outline on extras stays untouched.

Verified in dark mode: `borderTop: 1px rgb(39, 50, 64)`, `borderBottom: 1px rgb(39, 50, 64)`.
