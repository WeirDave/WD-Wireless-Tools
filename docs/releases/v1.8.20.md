Two rounds of Duplicates styling feedback in one release — Dave's phrase for the previous cut was "a muddy fucking mess."

Suite `v1.8.18 → v1.8.20` (v1.8.19 was never cut — its title-color revert is folded in here). WD Cloud Manager `v3.28 → v3.30`.

## What changed

### Cluster header (`.dup-head`)

- **Purple background stays** — that identity is fine on its own.
- **Purple border-left tip removed** — Dave: *"Take away the purple tip at the end of the card and just make the card be the purple background."*
- **Title text is default `--text` color** (near-white in dark, near-black in light) — Dave: *"maybe just make it the background but not the text too."* The purple background carries the identity; the type is normal.

### Row identity by SIDE (`.dup-item.side-cloud` / `.side-local`)

- **Removed the blue/green background tints** — they were making the page feel washed and muddy, especially where they overlapped with the amber extras.
- **Added colored TIPS on both left AND right edges of each row** — 4 px solid `--blue` for cloud rows, 4 px solid `--green` for local rows. Dave: *"put the tips on the ends of the cards to designate cloud and local and put them on both sides of the card with just the tips being the colors that we use."*
- Base padding compensated (`padding-left: 12px; padding-right: 12px`) so the row content still lines up now that the borders eat 4 px on each side.

### Extras (`.dup-item.is-extra`)

Unchanged — still uses the amber `box-shadow` inset outline. Now the outline sits INSIDE the blue/green side tips, which reads as a nested frame: outer color = which side (cloud/local), inner outline = "look at me, I'm an extra."

## Not touched (yet)

Dave mused *"I honestly don't even think that we should use the orange up top either but I'm not 100% sure"* about the amber Duplicate Projects tab active color. Left as-is until you commit.
